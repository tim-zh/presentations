monad laws

  • flatMap(of(x), f) = f(x) (Left identity)
  • flatMap(f(x), of) = f(x) (Right identity)
  • flatMap(h(x), y => flatMap(g(y), f)) = flatMap(flatMap(h(x), g), f)
